# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions and reviews.
- Focus feedback on code, design, and outcomes rather than people.
- Assume good intent and ask for clarification before escalating.
- Share context and evidence when reporting issues.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks, insults, trolling, or repeated hostile behavior.
- Publishing private information without explicit consent.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional environment.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior through GitHub Security Advisories or repository maintainers.
Include links, timestamps, and relevant context when possible.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.
They may remove comments, commits, issues, or contributors that violate this Code of Conduct.
